While this was a team effort, my primary responsibility was the core gameplay systems—specifically the interactions between the fishing rod, bait, and fish. This was more than just coding mechanics; it was about creating a satisfying, intuitive interaction model in VR.

Core Responsibility

Design and implement the bait-fishing rod-fish interaction system that made catching fish feel natural and rewarding in VR space.
